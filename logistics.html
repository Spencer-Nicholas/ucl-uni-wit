Event Logistics


Date & Time
  • Wednesday 25th April 2018
  • Doors open at 9am for registration, with a formal start at 9.30am sharp. We will finish by 4.30pm
Location
  • Sackville Street Building, 58 Sackville Street, Manchester, M1 3WS. This is towards the North of the University campus.

    University of Manchester maps site: http://www.manchester.ac.uk/discover/maps/
    University of Manchester interactive map: http://www.manchester.ac.uk/discover/maps/interactive-map/
    University of Manchester downloadable map: http://documents.manchester.ac.uk/display.aspx?DocID=6507

  • The venue is a 7- to 10-minute walk from Manchester Piccadilly station, and we recommend using public transport to travel to the university. There may be parking available in the Charles Street Car Park, just 3 minutes from the venue, but spaces are small and fill fast. You can find more about parking options and prices here: http://www.estates.manchester.ac.uk/services/operationalservices/carparking/
Dress Code
  • We would recommend smart casual or business casual
Social Media
  • If you'd like to tweet about the event, please use the hashtag #IBMWIT. You could also follow @InterConnectIBM on Twitter.
  • An invite to a Slack workspace will follow soon.
Laptops
  • Laptops will not be required for this event
Learning Credits
  • Those who register at the event will be awarded credit towards YourLearning via EventCentral
